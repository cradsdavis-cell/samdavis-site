The problem

AI looks like a wave. Most owners are bracing for it.

Most business owners I talk to are doing quiet maths on AI. It can write your emails and plan your week, so what's left for the person who built the business? Some are overwhelmed by the tools. Plenty are just looking away and hoping it passes.

It isn't going to pass. But you can ride it rather than get bowled over by it.

I teach you to build your own AI assistant, one that knows your business and does your admin. You steer. It paddles.

What you'll build

An assistant that actually knows you.

Drafts in your voice.
Your phrasing, your rhythms, your signature moves — so the replies read like you actually wrote them.
Knows your people.
Every client, partner and lead has a page. It pulls the relationship history before you reply — tiered by closeness.
Catches what's slipping.
It surfaces the follow-up you forgot, the loop you left open, the thread going cold — so nothing falls through.
