§ What you're looking at

Nothing on that screen was typed in by hand.

Each card is fed by the system described on the overview page. Small scheduled jobs sweep the channels; deterministic readers write the state; the dashboard just draws it. My real one runs on my own machine with my real clients, money, and threads in it, which is exactly why this replica runs on invented data instead.

Overnight sweeps

A small fleet of scheduled jobs reads inbox, calendar, Slack, WhatsApp, the client tracker, and Stripe while I sleep. Each writes what it found as plain structured state.

One honest state

Every card renders from the same files the AI reads and writes all day: the wiki that holds people, decisions, deadlines, and money. No second copy to drift out of date.

Human on the sends

The system drafts, flags, and reconciles on its own. Nothing outbound leaves without a yes from me. The dashboard shows the state; I stay the one who acts on it.
