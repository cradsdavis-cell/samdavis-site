Coaching Block

Build alongside me. Four sessions, $500.

For founders who want the assistant installed into their actual workflow, in real time, with me alongside. Session one establishes context. Best for: technical or semi-technical founders who want hands-on learning while we install. Sessions 2–4 booked after Session 1 so we pace to your reality.

EA Basic Build

I install your assistant. One week, $2,000.

For founders who want the assistant installed quickly and don't want to be in the build process. Best for: non-technical founders who want the end state, not the building process. Two meetings total, asynchronous in between.
